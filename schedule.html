Working Groups

Although you will be working through the materials on your own, in this lesson we've split you into working groups - each consisting of four people. The groups give you the opportunity to discuss the lesson, and any problems, with a small group of people from the course.

Before you start working through the materials, connect to your group (see links below) and say hello. Keep the channel open during the lesson and talk to your group (either via video or through text) as you work through the materials. Let your group know if you have any problems or any other thoughts on the lesson.

Helpers will drop into the groups throughout the lesson to answer questions.
